About Us

Our Story

Yellow Leaf Hammocks was founded with a simple goal: to create the most comfortable and durable hammocks in the world while empowering artisan weavers. What began as a passion project has grown into a globally recognized brand, loved by relaxation enthusiasts and eco-conscious consumers alike.

Every Yellow Leaf Hammock is handwoven by skilled artisans, providing them with a sustainable livelihood. We are proud to combine beautiful craftsmanship with ethical practices, ensuring that each hammock is not only a product of quality but also a force for good.

Empowering women.

Creating jobs for women is especially important for community transformation. Research shows that women will spend up to 90% of their income on health, nutrition, and education for their families.

Our Mission

At Yellow Leaf Hammocks, our mission is to foster relaxation and sustainability through high-quality, ethically-made products. We believe in the power of rest and the importance of supporting communities around the world. By choosing Yellow Leaf, you’re not just investing in comfort; you’re investing in a brighter future for artisan weavers and their families.
